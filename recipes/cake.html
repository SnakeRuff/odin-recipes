Vanilla Cake

Description

Every baker needs a solid vanilla cake recipe in their repertoire, and today's recipe is just that. It's soft, plush, and perfectly moist. Not to be confused with my white cake, this recipe is a beautiful pale golden color and distinctly vanilla flavored. It pairs beautifully with any frosting, although a traditional chocolate frosting is my favorite finish!

Ingredients

  • Oil and butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Buttermilk
